# Peanut butter and jelly sandwich
Amazing sandwich

## Ingredients

* two pieces of bread
* 1 tbs peanut butter
* 1 tbs jelly of any flavor

# low-carb option
* substitute bread with lettuce. 

## Directions
* spread peanut butter and jelly on one piece of bread
* put the other piece of bread on top

## Serving Suggestion
put on a plate
